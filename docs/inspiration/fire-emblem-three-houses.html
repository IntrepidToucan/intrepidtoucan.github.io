Fire Emblem: Three Houses

Playthrough Journal

2022-02-22

Started a new game once again. This time, I’m going to try to sit through all the cutscenes and listen to as much of the in-game dialogue as I can stand rather than skipping through everything as quickly as possible. The voice acting and writing in this game are so well done that it’s a shame not to enjoy and appreciate them fully.

Chose to lead the Black Eagles house under Edelgard, as usual! I want to complete all the storylines eventually, but I need to finish at least one first. I’m lousy at following through with video games, regardless of how excellent they are.

Spent almost three hours tonight getting to the first bandit mission (exploring the monastery each month is such a time-consuming pain!). I need to start respecting the timer I set to limit my video game time...
